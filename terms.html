Article 5 - Payment process

Participants can pay the registration fee through their personalized page. They can access this page by using a link included in their registration confirmation email. The registration fee differs with regard to the date of payment (e.g. early or late registration) and the status of the customer (e.g. reduced student fee). If the participant pays the wrong amount of registration fee, the Organizer can deny the participant attendance at the conference until the correct amount is paid in full.

Participants can choose between two methods of payment:

  1. Bank transfer, using the information provided on the payment page. The payment is confirmed after the correct amount is received on the account stated on payment page.
  2. Credit card payment. For the credit card data entry, the customer is redirected to a secure server of the payment processing bank. Payment can be made with the following credit or debit cards: VISA, VISA Electron, MASTERCARD and MAESTRO. The participant’s credit card will be debited right after a successful competition of the payment process.

Participant agrees to receive invoices for their payments referring to SICGT in electronic form.

Participant will receive the payment receipt by email in 48 hours after the confirmation of payment and must bring it to the conference venue as a proof of payment. The payment invoice will be presented to the customer after the conference.

We understand that cancellations are sometimes necessary. Participants may receive a refund for cancellation, the amount depends on the cancellation date:

  • Cancellation requests made before May 5, 2023 – The registration fee is refunded, minus 50€ reduced due to administration costs.

If you have to cancel your participation and would like to be refunded, please send by e-mail a written request to SiCGT23@fmf.uni-lj.si before June 14, 2023.
